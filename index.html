About me

Naman Shukla is a data science researcher and core team member at Deepair.

His research focuses on dynamic pricing with contextual information. He develops machine learning algorithms and statistical methods, analyzes revenue management data and engineers computational techniques. His work has appeared in special editon Journal for Applied Analytics in Institute for Operations Research and the Management Sciences (INFORMS) and Airline Group of the International Federation of Operational Research Societies (AGIFORS). He has presented in conference on Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI), Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML) and Special Interest Group on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (SIGKDD). His most recent work includes developing machine learning solutions to incorporate shape constraints for fair and reliable deep leaning models.

Naman's machine learning solutions have been deployed by Deepair at major airlines and travel prvides in Europe and Asia. His development responsibilities is divided into three roles: machine learning engineering, research infrastructure management and software engineering. He developed software tools and frameworks that is used by in-house data science teams and researchers at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, Imperial College London and University of Notre Dame. His team at Deepair has New Distribution Capability (NDC) level 4 certification by International Air Transport Association's (IATA).

Naman received his masters of science in industrial engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, IL. He is a recipient of japan student services organization (JASSO) scholarship for innovation program by University of Tokyo, Japan. He was awarded computational biology scholarship for associate researcher at Ritsumeikan University, Japan. He has a bachelor of technology in chemical engineering and minor in entrepreneurship both with academic excellence award from Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad, India.
